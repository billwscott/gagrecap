The New Gag Recap Newsletter is put out monthly by the gag writer Van B. Scott III.

We welcome comments, corrections, information about markets. Whatever you want to discuss about cartooning and writing. Tips about cartooning/writing, useful books, websites. Whatever!

Cartoonists willing to work with writers: Please send contact information, special requests, etc. and it will be listed in future issues. Writers contact information and credits will also be listed here for interested writers wanting to work with cartoonists.
