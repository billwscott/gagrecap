For many years the Gag Recap was a staple for cartoonist/gag writers everywhere. Unfortunately with the passing of Bill Keough the magazine stopped circulation a few years ago. I am excited to announce "The New" Gag Recap!

The New Gag Recap is delivered monthly by email. It is a comprehensive roundup of the latest cartoons published. The New Gag Recap will also include information on new and existing markets for cartoonists and writers.
